3+ Years of Creativity

Bringing Stories to Life with Art

I’m Namrata Powar, a self-taught contemporary artist specializing in acrylic painting. My artistic journey is rooted in curiosity, emotion, and an enduring fascination with the way colors and textures can transform a blank canvas into a story.

Each of my works is a reflection of mood and meaning — an exploration of harmony between light, form, and feeling. I draw inspiration from everyday moments, nature’s subtleties, and the quiet poetry found in simplicity.

Beyond painting, I channel my creativity into handcrafted candles and bespoke home décor pieces, where art meets functionality. These creations celebrate the beauty of handmade craftsmanship and the warmth of personal touch.

Through every medium I explore, my goal is to evoke emotion, spark connection, and bring an artistic essence into the spaces we live in.
